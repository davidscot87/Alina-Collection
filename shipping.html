Delivery Policies

How much does shipping cost?

Inside Kathmandu Valley: NPR 100
Outside Kathmandu Valley: NPR 150 - 250 depending on location.
Fast shipping available on all orders!

How long will it take to get my order?

Inside Kathmandu Valley: 1 - 2 business days.
Major Cities (Pokhara, Butwal, Biratnagar, etc.): 2 - 4 business days.
Remote areas: 4 - 7 business days.

Do you offer express delivery?

Yes, we offer same-day delivery inside Kathmandu Valley for orders placed before 11:00 AM. Additional charges may apply.

How can I change my shipping address?

If your order hasn't been shipped yet, you can change your address by contacting our support team via WhatsApp or Phone immediately.
