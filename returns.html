Returns & Exchanges

Our goal is your complete satisfaction. If you're not happy, we're here to help.

Our Return Policy

How many days do I have to return an item?

We offer a 7-day easy return policy. You can return or exchange any item within 7 days of delivery, provided it is unused and in its original condition.

What items are non-returnable?

For hygiene reasons, innerwear, socks, and jewelry cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or defective.

How do I initiate an exchange?

Contact our support team via WhatsApp or Email with your Order ID and the reason for exchange. Once approved, our courier will pick up the item and deliver the replacement.

When will I get my refund?

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. Refunds are typically issued via Bank Transfer or cash arrangement.
