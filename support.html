Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

You can track your order by visiting the "Account Dashboard" and clicking on "Order History". You will also receive an email with tracking details once your order is shipped.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy for most items. The product must be in its original condition with tags intact. Please visit our returns portal for more details.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we primarily serve Nepal. However, we are expanding our services. Please contact our support team for specific international delivery requests.

How can I pay for my order?

We accept various payment methods including Cash on Delivery, eSewa, Khalti, and major Credit/Debit cards through our secured payment gateway.
